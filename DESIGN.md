# Order From Drift — creative brief (locked)

Working title for the showcase rebuild. Full brief generated by Opus (Fable was out of credits at build time — noted for the guide page).

Aurora gradient: `linear-gradient(120deg, #28E0C8 0%, #6C4CF0 100%)`.

## Typography
- Display: Clash Display (Fontshare, free) — big cinematic statements
- Body/UI: Manrope (Google Fonts, variable) — kept from legacy brand for continuity
- Mono/data: JetBrains Mono (Google Fonts) — numbers, status, VBS element codes

## 3D concept
"The Drift Field" — Three.js Points + custom shader. Base positions + curl-noise offset, mixed toward 7-node lattice targets via `uOrder` uniform; two ribbon lines diverge/converge via `uDrift` uniform. Both uniforms driven by scroll progress. Additive blending + UnrealBloomPass. Reduced-motion fallback: static teal-lattice still.

## Narrative arc (6 beats)
1. Cold Open — "Somewhere, right now, a plant is running on someone's memory."
2. The Overload — "One head. Seven regulatory elements. Ten mailboxes. It doesn't scale."
3. The Drift — "Documents say one thing. The shop floor does another. The gap between them is your risk."
4. The Reframe — "Compliance was never memory-work. It's infrastructure."
5. The Command Center — "One system. Seven elements. All green. Always."
6. Audit-Ready — "When the inspector walks in, you don't scramble. You just open it."

## What NOT to do
No Tron grids/hexagon HUD/wireframe globes/digital rain. Dashboard beat stays ruthlessly legible (max 7 cards, no violet text on navy). Motion only where it deepens drift→order story.

North star: *watching entropy get organized by something intelligent — cold and vast until it resolves into warmth and order.*
